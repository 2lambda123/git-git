When combining multiple `--contains` and `--no-contains` filters, only
references that contain at least one of the `--contains` cummits and
contain none of the `--no-contains` cummits are shown.

When combining multiple `--merged` and `--no-merged` filters, only
references that are reachable from at least one of the `--merged`
cummits and from none of the `--no-merged` cummits are shown.
